# Speedcubing Canada Membership Policy

Uses of the words “must”, “must not”, “should”, “should not” and “may” match [RFC 2119](https://www.ietf.org/rfc/rfc2119.txt).

Speedcubing Canada’s Board of Directors reserves the right to modify this document with a majority of not less than two-thirds (2/3) of Speedcubing Canada’s Board of Directors vote in favour of the modification.

- Membership in the Corporation is granted to the following individuals:
  - Speedcubing Canada Directors
  - Speedcubing Canada Officers
  - World Cube Association Delegates who are assigned to Canada (excluding Trainee Delegates)
- The Board of Directors may appoint membership to an individual by a majority of not less than two-thirds (2/3) of the directors
- Individuals may apply for membership and will be accepted by a majority of not less than two-thirds (2/3) of the directors using this form: [https://forms.gle/x78YBEDanfWaVNi26](https://forms.gle/x78YBEDanfWaVNi26)
