# Annual Members' Meeting - Speedcubing Canada

Date: January 14, 2026

Total number of Members of the Corporation: 36
Number of Members of the Corporation present: 23
Number of Members of the Corporation present via proxy: 5
Quorum present: Yes (28/36)

Location: Online via Google Meet video conference

## Voting method

Votes were collected via the "raise hand" function in Google Meet.

## Speedcubing Canada By-Laws v1.5 Confirmation

- A full changelog of the By-Laws since the last Annual Members' Meeting can be viewed [on GitHub](https://github.com/Speedcubing-Canada/docs-speedcubing-canada/pull/35/files#diff-4730a3f9b6c569e2005c3da7d55b31b3cd5464335e537b62e931d4939501860a)
- Summary of changes: 
  - Referenced the Speedcubing Canada Supported Events Policy, Speedcubing Canada Reimbursement Policy, Speedcubing Canada Logo Usage Policy, and Speedcubing Canada Membership Policy as supplementary to the by-laws
- Confirmation votes:
  - 0/28 present Members voted to amend [v1.5 of the Speedcubing Canada By-Laws](https://www.speedcubingcanada.org/documents/by-laws-v1.5.pdf)
  - 0/28 present Members voted to reject [v1.5 of the Speedcubing Canada By-Laws](https://www.speedcubingcanada.org/documents/by-laws-v1.5.pdf)
  - 24/28 present Members voted to **confirm** [v1.5 of the Speedcubing Canada By-Laws](https://www.speedcubingcanada.org/documents/by-laws-v1.5.pdf)
    - There were some technical difficulties with the "raise hand" function during voting, however, the tallied votes were more than sufficient to confirm the by-laws

## Changes to the Board of Directors

- Abdullah Gulab stepped down from the Speedcubing Canada Board of Directors mid-2025 to join the World Cube Association Board of Directors
- The two-year terms of Jonathan Esparaz, Kristopher De Asis, Liam Orovec, and Tarandeep Mittal ended at the conclusion of this Annual Members' Meeting
- Prior to this Annual Members' Meeting, applications were opened to Members, Officers, and Directors of Speedcubing Canada to apply for election or re-election to the Board of Directors
- Kristopher De Asis chose to apply for re-election
- Jonathan Esparaz, Liam Orovec, and Tarandeep Mittal chose not to apply for re-election
- Alex Mutch and Joanne Chew chose to apply for election
- The applications of Kristopher De Asis, Alex Mutch (Alexander Tyler Mutch), and Joanne Chew (Joanne Wai Fun Chiu) were accepted by a majority of Members of Speedcubing Canada to serve a minimum one-year term with the option to extend to a full two-year term at the discretion of each Director (re-election required after two years as per the by-laws)
